# 🧬 GFOFS – Food Classification Tables (v5)
## Gut Food Optimized Filters Set (IBS + Celiac)

> Governed by: **GFOFS_rules_v1.0.md**

---

## Assumptions
- Whole, minimally processed foods only
- All foods listed are:
  - Gluten-free
  - Low FODMAP at normal culinary portions
  - Low / acceptable prolamines
- Highly processed foods are excluded by default
- Tables include only **reliably safe, low-failure foods**
- **Foods are ordered by ascending overall gut risk**

---

## Global Preparation Note
Where applicable (e.g. legumes), foods are assumed to be **soaked for a prolonged period and pressure-cooked**, which **reduces lectin activity to generally minimal levels**.

---

- **Lectins:** Low / Moderate / High *(informational)*
- **Fiber:** Low / Moderate / High *(informational)*
- **Histamine (content):** Low / Moderate / High *(contextual)*
- **Histamine interaction:** Low / Moderate / High *(contextual; liberator-type risk)*

## Final Note – Meal Timing & Intermittent Fasting

Some individuals with gut disorders report symptom improvement when using **intermittent fasting**, including **one meal a day (OMAD)**.

Intermittent fasting is **optional**, highly individual, and should be tested
personally. Food choice and preparation remain more important than meal frequency.
